About Me

I am originally from Montana. I have spent most my life growing up and attending school in Washington state. Currently I split my time between Tacoma and Bellingham. Over the past several years I have owned small businesses which includes my own products and crafts. Over the past two years I have spent most my time combining my businesses into one in Tacoma. I currently own a therapy garden and non-profit gift shop that helps subsidize safe/sober housing for single/independent women. During my free time I love to play frisbee with my dog Wild. I am currently is pursuing my education in Full Stack Web Development.
