About Me

Jennifer Detmering is originally from Montana. She spent most her life growing up and attending school in Washington state. She currently splits her time between Tacoma and Bellingham. Due to the every changing job market Jenny has had to be flexible and adaptable to maintain employment. Over the past several years she has owned several small businesses which included her own products and inventions. Over the past two years she has chosen to spend most her time combining her businesses into one in Tacoma. She currently owns a therapy garden and gift shop that helps subsidize safe/sober housing for single/independent women. During her free time she loves to play frisbee with her dog Wild. Jenny currently is puruing her education in Full Stack Web Development.
